TerrorCTF

Contest Information

Start: Saturday, February 3rd, 2024 at 2pm PST

End: Sunday, February 4th, 2024 at 5pm PST

Discord Server: https://discord.gg/3AHZweXTCc

Contest Rules

  1. Don't talk with other teams about the problems during the contest (i.e. don't cheat).
  2. Teams can have up to 4 people.
  3. Don't attempt to break the main website itself.
  4. Don't attempt to break out of the jail on the images.
  5. Have fun!

How to Participate

Talk with up to 3 other people to form a team (or you can solo but it might not be as fun). Each person should create an account. Then, one person should go to their profile, then press "Create a Team". A team ID will be generated. Other participants can go their profile, press "Join a Team", and type in that team ID to join the team.

On the day of the contest, press the "problems" link. You will see all the problems for the contest.
